01. About Me

Hi! My name is Peter Do (pronounced "Doh!" like Homer Simpson) and I am an aspiring full stack web developer. My interest in web development started back in high school when I would customize my Tumblr and MySpace pages. Little did I know I was using HTML/CSS to do so!

I hope to complete it in the near future and join the world of professional web development.

Outside of web development, I am an avid disc golfer and enjoy the outdoors as much as I can. I also enjoy playing video games on my PC, getting together with friends, and playing music!
